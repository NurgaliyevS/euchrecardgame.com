About Euchre Card Game

Euchre is a classic trick-taking card game that's especially popular in the Midwest and parts of Canada. This online version lets you play the traditional Euchre card game against computer opponents.

How to Play Euchre

Euchre is played with 24 cards (9, 10, J, Q, K, A of each suit). Players form two teams of two, sitting across from their partners. The goal is to win at least 3 of the 5 tricks in each hand. The first team to reach 10 points wins!
